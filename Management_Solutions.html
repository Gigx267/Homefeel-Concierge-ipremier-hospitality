Key Components of an Apartment Management Solution

Tenant Management:
Screening: Conducting thorough background checks to select reliable tenants.
Leasing: Handling lease agreements, rent collection, and security deposits.
Communication: Maintaining effective communication with tenants to address concerns and maintain positive relationships.

Property Maintenance:

Regular Inspections: Conducting regular inspections to identify and address maintenance issues.
Maintenance Requests: Promptly responding to tenant maintenance requests.
Vendor Management: Contracting and managing reliable vendors for repairs and maintenance.

Financial Management:

Accounting: Tracking income, expenses, and financial performance.
Budgeting: Creating and managing budgets to ensure financial stability.
Reporting: Providing regular financial reports to property owners.

Legal Compliance:


Regulations: Ensuring compliance with local, state, and federal regulations.
Fair Housing Laws: Adhering to fair housing laws to prevent discrimination.
Lease Enforcement: Enforcing lease terms to protect the property owner's interests.

Emergency Management:

Preparedness: Developing emergency preparedness plans.
Response: Responding to emergencies promptly and effectively.

Property Management Guidelines

Monitoring

  • Create a group chat for security, reception, police, facility management, property managers, and supervisors.
  • Use this group to monitor short-let activities at the Towers.

Bookings

  • Guests must provide full names, number of guests (limit 4/6), and ID copies.
  • Bookings must be made in the group chat before entry.
  • Include stay duration (min. 3 days), vehicle info, and check-in/check-out dates.
  • Guests without group chat bookings will be denied entry.

Rules and Guidelines

  • Property managers must place residency rules in apartments.
  • Collect security deposits.
  • Supervisors must know guest whereabouts.
  • Prohibit drugs, parties, loud noise, and weapons.
  • Issue guest passes with manager's logo and apartment number.
  • Deny guests with criminal records or illegal activities.
  • Report violence to police.
  • Register visitors at the concierge.
  • Maintain payment records for law enforcement inquiries.
  • Agents are responsible for guest-related crimes.

House Rules (Sapphire Shortlets)

  • Check-in at 2 PM, check-out by 12 PM.
  • Early/late check-ins subject to availability, may require prior night's booking.
  • Management may remove guests who overstay.
  • Guests are responsible for belongings.
  • Lost items will be held for 90 days, returned at owner's expense.

Fire Safety Policy

  • The Apartments are fully equipped with smoke and heat detectors. While there are emergency evacuation plans such as fire extinguishers and fire hose reels on every floor.

  • No Pet Policy

    No animal or pet of any kind shall be kept in the apartment for any amount of time.

  • Refund Policy

    Please be informed that all payments made are non-refundable if a guest fails to show up on their check in date.

  • Damage to Property

    The guest will be held responsible for any loss or damage to the property caused by themselves, their guests, or any person for whom they are responsible   for. Guests shall not paint, wallpaper, alter or redecorate, change or install locks, install antenna or other equipment, screws, fastening devices, large nails, or adhesive materials, place signs, displays, or other exhibits, on or in any portion of   the apartment.

  • Smoking Policy

    WE OBSERVE A NON-SMOKING RULE POLICY INSIDE THE APARTMENT AT ALL TIMES. SMOKING OF ALL SORTS AND THE USE OF HARD DRUGS ARE HIGHLY PROHIBITED AS THE PENALTY FOR DEFAULTING THIS RULE IS IMMEDIATE EVICTION WITHOUT A REFUND.

  • Noise Policy

    The guest agrees not to cause or allow any noise or activity in the apartment which might disturb the peace and quiet of another guest and/or neighbor. Said noise or activity shall be a breach of this agreement.

  • Party Policy

    WE OBSERVE A NO PARTY RULE AT ALL TIMES. PARTIES OF ANY SORT ARE PROHIBITED FROM ALL OUR APARTMENTS. FAILURE TO ADHERE TO THIS RULE WOULD LEAD TO IMMEDIATE EVICTION FROM THE APARTMENT.
